### ğŸ“Š Dashboard Overview
The Power BI dashboard is organised into three pages:

**1) Sales Overview**
- **Visualisations:**
  -  **Sum of Sales and Profit Margin:** combined column and line chart highlighting total sales and profit margin by month.
    - The chart uses a dual-axis to show both metrics on the same view, allowing a comparison of total sales and profit.
  - **Sum of Sales by Group and Category**: a stacked column chart illustrating sales by Group, each stack represents a category.
  - **Sum of Quantity by Category**: total of items sold per category.
- **Interactivity:**
  - Filters that can be applied include category, month and group.
  - Slicers for year and region. <br> <br>
